Privacy Policy

SnackPet  ·  Effective date: June 20, 2026  ·  Last updated: June 20, 2026

Short summary: SnackPet does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on remote servers. All information (feeding schedules, device preferences) stays entirely on your device and on your pet feeder hardware.

This Privacy Policy explains what information SnackPet ("the App", "we", "our") accesses on your device, how it is used, and your rights. The App is a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) companion application for a smart cat feeder device, available on Android and iOS.

1. Information We Access

The App accesses the following types of information solely on your device to provide its core features:

Category Details Stored remotely?
Bluetooth device identifier The unique ID of your paired cat feeder, saved locally so the App can reconnect automatically on next launch. No — device only
Feeding schedule Meal times (hour, minute) and portion sizes (number of scoops) that you configure inside the App. Written directly to your feeder device over BLE. No — feeder hardware only
Device date & time Your phone's current date and time, sent to the feeder once at connection so scheduled meals fire at the correct time. No — feeder hardware only
Feeding history Historical meal records read from the feeder hardware and displayed in the History screen. No — read from feeder only

We do not access your location, camera, microphone, contacts, photos, or any other sensor or data store beyond what is listed above.

2. How We Use Information

  • Auto-reconnect — The saved device ID lets the App find and reconnect to your feeder automatically, without you having to scan every time.
  • Schedule management — Meal times and portion sizes you set are written to the feeder via BLE and read back to display your current schedule.
  • Clock synchronisation — Your device's date and time is sent to the feeder at connection so it keeps accurate meal timing.
  • History display — Past meal records are retrieved from the feeder and shown in the App's History screen for your reference.

No information is used for advertising, analytics, profiling, or any purpose other than controlling your feeder.

3. Data Storage

The only data the App persists locally is the BLE device identifier of your feeder, stored in the App's private data directory using Android DataStore / iOS equivalent on-device storage. This data is:

  • Never transmitted off your device.
  • Removed automatically when you uninstall the App.
  • Removable at any time via the App's Settings screen (disconnect / forget device).

4. Bluetooth Permissions

Android

  • BLUETOOTH_SCAN — scan for nearby BLE devices to find your feeder.
  • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT — establish and maintain the BLE connection.
  • BLUETOOTH_ADVERTISE — not used; declared for future feature compatibility only.
  • On Android 11 and below, ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION is required by the OS for BLE scanning. The App does not use or record your location.

iOS

  • NSBluetoothAlwaysUsageDescription — required by iOS to communicate with BLE peripherals. The App does not use background BLE except to reconnect when you open the App.

Bluetooth permissions are requested only when you first initiate a device scan. You can revoke them in your device's system settings at any time; doing so will disable App functionality.

5. Third-Party SDKs & Analytics

SnackPet does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking SDKs. The App has no network connectivity requirements and makes no outbound internet connections.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13 (COPPA) or under 16 (GDPR). Because no data is collected at all, no special handling for children is required.

7. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or process personal data on any server, there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, or delete. All App data resides on your own device and your feeder hardware, which you control entirely.

To remove all locally stored App data, uninstall the App or use the "Forget Device" option in the Settings screen. Clearing App data via your phone's system settings achieves the same result.

8. Security

Communication between the App and your feeder uses the Bluetooth Low Energy protocol with standard GATT security. Data is never transmitted over the internet. The device identifier stored locally is kept in the App's private, sandboxed storage and is not accessible to other apps.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, the new version will be published at the same URL and the "Last updated" date at the top will change. Significant changes will also be noted in the App Store / Google Play release notes. Continued use of the App after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
